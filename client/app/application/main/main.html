Three Pillars of Convenience Select




Convenience Select brings an industry leading, state-of-the-art registration and payments platform at a price that everyone can afford. Our platform allows us to offer a club our registration system and a flexible payments platform that can handle registration and tuition payments, private coaching fees, and any other fee you can throw at us.


The second pillar of Convenience Select is our reporting, designed to provide the club detailed information about where we are in the payments process with each and every family. Think of us as an extension of your team handling everything related to payments and keeping you in the loop at every point in the process.


We find that 3 of every 10 families will have a need to reach out to us with a question or concern about payments or perhaps with a request to schedule a custom payment. We love working with parents as we are parents ourselves and work tirelessly to support those parents via phone, email and chat on a 1:1 basis.

Wondering what the process looks like?
Well, here’s the Convenience Select playbook.


Once you’ve signed up, we call you to setup your teams and payment schedules.

Send parents to our site after your tryouts and parent meetings.

We handle all aspects of payments for you, including follow-up on missed payments and support calls.

We deposit your money immediately into the bank account you provided during sign-up and provide your reporting package monthly!
