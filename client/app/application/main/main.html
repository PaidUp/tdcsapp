Payments Platform + Support for Parents


Platform
PaidUp's easy to use online platform is flexible enough to meet the needs of both parents and leagues. Payments can be easily broken up into monthly, weekly or seasonal payments. If a parent needs to call an audible, we can adjust payments schedules on the fly for their convenience. You're always funded, parents are taken care of and athletes are always eligible.
Support
Teamwork isn't just found on the field, Our dedicated support team builds relationships with parents, so when they have a problem, they come to us - not to you, Our support team is as diverse as your team, with service available in English, Spanish and Portuguese. We also meticulously log every encounter and report back to you, so you know what's going on, without having to handle the money. Be a coach, not a customer service rep.

Wondering what the process looks like?
Well, here’s the PaidUp playbook.


Once you’ve signed up, we call you to setup your teams and payment schedules.

Send parents to our site after your tryouts and parent meetings.

We handle all aspects of payments for you, including follow-up on missed payments and support calls.

We deposit your money immediately into the bank account you provided during sign-up and provide your reporting package monthly!
