Crowdfunding is the practice of funding a project or campaign by raising monetary contributions from a large number of people, typically via the internet. SelectFund is the name of the crowdfunding platform built by Convenience Select. SelectFund campaigns are projects or services created by Convenience Select or you the team owner, to raise money to pay for the project or service. A campaign typically has a campaign goal, it could be people (e.g. 20 people must sign up to attend a tour of Fenway Park) or money (e.g. $200 to paint new lines on the field or $500 to play in a tournament). SelectFund sends campaigns are sent to parents (or they view them on our site) with details about the campaign and why they should participate. All campaigns have a date by which they need to be funded. SelectFund is all or nothing. Campaigns must be reach their goal within the time frame specified otherwise they are a no-go. SelectFund is free to use by anyone in your organization. Convenience Select charges 5% for any successfully funded campaign. Any campaigns funded with credit cards will incur 2.9% + $0.30 processing fees (standard Stripe interchange fees).
