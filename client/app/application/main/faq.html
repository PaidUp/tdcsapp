Pricing FAQs

How does Convenience Select pricing really work?

The prices for the packages listed are per athlete per month. For example, if your season is 4 months, then each athlete pays 4 x $15 (Gold Package) = $60 for the season.

Are you charging my team or the parents?

This is really up to you since Convenience Select supports both. We could add this as a Value Added Services fee to the season dues we collect or you could bake that into the seasons dues. We have seen a lot more success with teams that bake this into their season cost to parents. For example, charge $1,060 instead of $1,000 for a 4 month season.

Do I have to pay for Convenience Select in the off-season?

No. You only pay for the services while your season is going on. For example, if your season is from February to April, you are only paying for 3 months.

Payment Collection FAQs

What comes with the payment collection service from Convenience Select?

Convenience Select is your concierge. We want to do the boring stuff for you so you can stay focused on coaching and growing your business. With the payment collection service, we will setup your team, registration fees and collection schedule on our site. You then direct all parents to www.convenienceselect.com where they will sign up for an account and enter their debit/credit card information and let Convenience Select process the payments.

Are there any other fees for payment collections?

Convenience Select gets charges 2.9% + $0.30 per credit card that’s processed. We don’t make any money on these fees and pass them along to the teams. For example, if you purchase a package containing Payment Collections and your season dues per player are $1,000, Convenience Select would send $970.70 to your bank account ($1,000 x 2.9% = $29 + $0.30 = $29.30 credit card processing fees).

How quickly do I get the money if Convenience Select collects payments for me?

If parents pay with their credit card, you can receive your money within 48 hours. If parents pay with a bank account, you can receive your money within 3-6 business days.

Who does Convenience Select use for card processing?

Convenience Select partners with the leading credit card processing company around, Stripe. The best way for Convenience Select to keep its prices low is to let Stripe deal with all the regulatory parts of taking payments. As a result, Stripe requires that we setup an account for you with Stripe and that only takes a few minutes to do so through our site. You can always call Convenience Select to help you through it.

SelectFund FAQs

What is crowdfunding?

Crowdfunding is the practice of funding a project or campaign by raising monetary contributions from a large number of people, typically via the internet.

What is SelectFund?

SelectFund is the name of the crowdfunding platform built by Convenience Select.

What is a SelectFund campaign?

SelectFund campaigns are projects or services created by Convenience Select or you the team owner, to raise money to pay for the project or service. A campaign typically has a campaign goal, it could be people (e.g. 20 people must sign up to attend a tour) or money (e.g. $200 to purchase a new pitching machine or $500 to play in a tournament).

How does SelectFund work?

SelectFund sends campaigns are sent to parents (or they view them on our site) with details about the campaign and why they should participate. All campaigns have a date by which they need to be funded. SelectFund is all or nothing. Campaigns must be reach their goal within the time frame specified otherwise they are a no-go.

What kind of campaigns can I create with SelectFund?

You can create self-managed campaigns, where Convenience Select let’s you take matters into your own hands. Your organization can create the campaigns and share that with your audience to get them funded.

What campaigns are prohibited with SelectFund?

All campaigns should be related to your sports organization. We prohibit projects that are illegal, heavily regulated, or potentially dangerous for backers are not allowed.
  • Any item claiming to cure, treat, or prevent an illness or condition (whether via a device, app, book, nutritional supplement, or other means).
  • Contests, coupons, gambling, and raffles.
  • Energy food and drinks.
  • Offensive material (e.g., hate speech, encouraging violence against others, etc).
  • Political fundraising.
  • Pornographic material.
  • Drugs, nicotine, tobacco, vaporizers and related paraphernalia.
  • Weapons, replicas of weapons, and weapon accessories.
  • If your project involves something illegal, heavily regulated, or potentially dangerous for backers and it is not on this list, please contact us before starting your project.

What does SelectFund cost?

SelectFund is free to use by anyone in your organization. Convenience Select charges 5% for any successfully funded campaign. Any campaigns funded with credit cards will incur 2.9% + $0.30 processing fees (standard Stripe interchange fees). Campaigns that are created but do not reach their funding goal incur no charges.
